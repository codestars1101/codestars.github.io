As a group, we contacted Mars Academy to develop a robotics project and asked
them to help us get a robot. We were warmly welcomed by Mars academy and provided
with the best conditions for this homework. Then we planned what we would do in the
robotics project. We got acquainted with the robot and wrote codes for it to follow
some instructions.

The first acquaintance with the robot and seeing its operations gave
us an idea of this project. Finally, All of us realized how fun this project is and how
important it is for us.
